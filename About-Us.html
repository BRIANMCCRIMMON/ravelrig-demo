Who We Are

Ravelrig Riding for the Disabled (RDA) is a volunteer-led charity based in the beautiful countryside of Balerno, just outside Edinburgh. We provide therapeutic horse riding opportunities to children and adults with physical and learning disabilities. Since opening our gates in 1985, we’ve welcomed hundreds of riders, each with their own story, abilities, and goals. Our sessions go beyond riding — they build strength, confidence, independence, and joy.

What We Do

We offer weekly riding sessions tailored to individual needs, led by qualified instructors and supported by a dedicated team of volunteers. Riders benefit from improved balance, coordination, and wellbeing — all while forming meaningful connections with our specially trained horses.

Our Values

At Ravelrig RDA, our values shape everything we do — in the arena, in the stables, and in our community. 

They reflect the inclusive, empowering, and collaborative spirit that makes our charity so special, and guide us as we support our riders, care for our horses, and work together as a team.

Inclusion

Everyone is welcome and supported to achieve their best

Empowerment

We help individuals develop confidence and independence

Partnership

Horses, volunteers, and riders work together in harmony

Community

We are stronger together

Our Community

At Ravelrig, you’ll find a vibrant, inclusive community made up of riders, families, volunteers, supporters, and of course, our much-loved horses. Whether helping in the arena, caring for horses, or fundraising, every person plays a part in making what we do possible. We are proud to be part of Riding for the Disabled Association UK, a national network of over 400 groups, and to be a trusted source of support and positivity in our local area.
