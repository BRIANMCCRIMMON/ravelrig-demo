Welcome to Ravelrig RDA

We are a volunteer-led charity offering therapeutic horse riding sessions to children and adults with physical and learning disabilities. 

Based in the beautiful surroundings of Ravelrig, we aim to create a space where our riders can build strength, confidence, and joy — all with the help of our incredible horses and volunteers.

What Makes Us Special

Ravelrig Riding for the Disabled is about so much more than horses — it’s about creating a safe, welcoming space where individuals of all abilities can grow, connect, and thrive.

Therapeutic Horse Riding

We provide specialist horse riding sessions that promote physical, emotional, and social wellbeing for children and adults with disabilities.

Inclusive Community


Ravelrig is a welcoming, supportive space where riders, volunteers, and families come together to create life-changing experiences.

Volunteer-Led Support


Our dedicated team of volunteers plays a vital role in delivering safe, personalised sessions and maintaining our stables and grounds.

Personal Growth & Confidence

Riders build balance, strength, and coordination — but just as importantly, confidence, independence, and joy through connection with our horses.
